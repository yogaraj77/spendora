# Spindora - Expense Tracking Website

Welcome to Spendora, your ultimate solution for tracking expenses across multiple countries. Whether you're in India, the USA, UK, or Europe, Spindora simplifies the process of managing your finances. Here's how you can get started:

#live working model
https://yogaraj77.github.io/spendora/

## Features

### Multi-Country Support
Easily track expenses for different countries including India, USA, UK, and Europe.

### Select Country
Use the dropdown menu to select your country.

### Expense Categories
Choose from various categories like Expense, Income, or Savings.

### Expense Details
Enter the name of the expense, the amount, and a description.

### Submit Expense
Once you have filled out the information, click the submit button to add the expense to your history.

### Transaction History
View your past transactions in the "down expense history" section.

## How to Use

1. **Select Country:** Use the dropdown menu to select your country.
2. **Choose Category:** Select the appropriate category for your expense (Expense, Income, Savings).
3. **Enter Details:** Fill in the name of the expense, the amount, and a brief description.
4. **Submit:** Click the submit button to add the expense to your history.
5. **View History:** Access your past transactions in the "down expense history" section.
